**Manager – Market Operations**  
  
The market operations team is responsible for operating the markets across the island of Ireland that allow generators and supply companies to buy and sell electricity ahead of the delivery date and subsequently the calculation of prices that apply for the real time balancing of supply and demand. This includes the operation of the day-ahead and intraday markets and the balancing market on a 365-day basis. We also operate the Guarantees of Origin issuing process for Ireland and Fuel Mix Disclosure processes for the SEM.    
   
This is a key role operating within a dynamic section of the Chief Operations and Asset Management Office. Reporting to the Head of Market Operations, the successful applicant will provide senior level leadership and support across the team and will be responsible for motivating and leading a team of circa. 12 people and co-ordinating the work of assigned staff. They will also work closely with our Belfast based market operations manager and team to ensure seamless operations of the markets.    
   
The Market Operations Manager will be responsible for coordinating the day to day trading operation processing activities in line with agreed procedures and timelines.  The manager will also be responsible for coordinating and managing the guarantees of origin and fuel mix disclosure processes in market operations.   
   
The role holder will take a lead role in ensuring agreed standards are adopted by the team and embedded into all business processes and activities.    
   
Along with strong team leadership abilities, the role requires excellent team working, organisational and communications skills. The role holder will manage goal setting and performance reviews for team members and will be responsible for coaching and facilitating staff to proactively respond to business issues.     
   
This role will provide the successful applicant with the opportunity to enhance their knowledge, skills and leadership capabilities in an interesting and dynamic environment. The critical nature of the work we do, and the experience it offers, provides exciting career opportunity.     
   
The role will be be based in Dublin with travel to Belfast from time to time to co-ordinate with our Belfast based market operations team. The market operations manager will also need to support the market oncall panel typically 1 week in every 6.    
   
**Key Responsibilities:** 

- Coordinate the work of assigned staff based on the needs of the business. 

- Plan and allocate team resources to meet priorities and manage issues, coach staff in issue resolution and act as an escalation point  

- Support the design and implement of appropriate KPIs and operational targets for the team, including the escalation of risks and issues 

- Coordinate and manage the schedule of trading operations activities to support the key operational activities for the market operations desk in line with the SEMOpx Rules and the Trading and Settlement Code  

- Manage the goal setting and performance management for team members andl be responsible for coaching and developing staff.  

- Ensure the Market Codes, Rules and licence obligations are met in accordance with an appropriate level of corporate governance.  

- Support effective engagement with both external and internal stakeholders including regulatory authorities and market participants by using the teams and/or their own knowledge and experience. 

- Work closely with our Belfast based market operations manger and team to ensure efficient and effective operation of the market processes. 

- Suppport the consultation, review, co-ordination and implementation of future changes to systems and processes by using the teams and/or their own knowledge and experience. 

- Ensure the right level of subject matter expertise and support is developed in the team to deal with queries and operational activities  

- Lead ongoing projects to developer operational excellence including process improvement and training activity 

- Contribute to the strategic development of the Market Operations team and the Operations and Asset Management Directorate  

- Coordinate the management of audits.
