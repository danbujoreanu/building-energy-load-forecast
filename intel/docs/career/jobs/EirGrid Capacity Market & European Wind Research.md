## 2. North Sea Summit 2026 - Offshore Wind Investment Pact

### Key Announcement (January 26, 2026)
**€1 TRILLION Investment Pact** for offshore wind in the North Sea

### Participating Countries
- Ireland
- France
- Belgium
- Netherlands
- Luxembourg
- Germany
- Denmark
- Norway (non-EU)

### Key Commitments
- **300 GW offshore wind by 2050** (reaffirmed from 2023 pledge)
- Turn the North Sea into "the green power plant of Europe"
- More evenly distributed investment across participating countries

### Current Status
- As of early 2026: **37 GW** offshore wind installed across Europe
- Boston Consulting Group analysis on North Sea potential

### Relevance to VIOTAS
- **Grid Flexibility Demand:** 300 GW of variable wind requires massive flexibility services
- **Interconnector Development:** North Sea grid will connect Ireland to European markets
- **Long-Term Growth:** Validates 2030-2035 market expansion thesis
