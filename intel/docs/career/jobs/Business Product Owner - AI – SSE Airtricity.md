# Business Product Owner - AI – SSE Airtricity

## **About the job**

Base Location: You'll be expected to spend 50% of your working week in the Dublin office


​
Salary: €65,300 - €85,000 + performance-related bonus + a range of benefits to support your
finances, wellbeing and family.​
​
[Working Pattern: Permanent | Full Time | Flexible First](https://www.linkedin.com/redir/redirect/?url=https%3A%2F%2Fcareers%2Esse%2Ecom%2Fflexible-working&urlhash=mRLD&isSdui=true) options available​
​
The role

As a Business Product Owner, the focus is on maximising business value and return on investment
to achieve business goals. In this role, with a focus on our AI Digital Care and Support products, you
will have responsibility to lead the adaptation of our core product set, to incorporate AI and its
potential into customer-facing and internal solutions.​
​
You will


●​ define and communicate the AI product vision, roadmap, strategy, and backlog of work
regularly, throughout the product’s development lifecycle (from discovery phase to design,
development, testing and ongoing performance operations and monitoring once live)
●​ collaborate with cross-functional stakeholders, and act as the voice of the business
throughout the product development lifecycle from ideation to execution and launch,
ensuring timely delivery of meaningful AI products.
●​ support Agile delivery through our core Technical Platform Teams by being a single point of
contact from the business, understanding the business areas unique needs, and considering
customer experience, user experience, digital analytics and other supporting stakeholders
●​ work on business cases that helps prioritise requirements, features and user stories based
on their business value and risks.
You have

●​ experience working across the AI product function including experience in agile product
delivery within the scope of Data, Architecture, UX, Mobile App and Web strategy
●​ a proven history of having built successful AI products at scale, and knowledge of
automation technologies within large organisations including experience in Agile or Scrum
methodologies
●​ a unique blend of business and technical savvy as well as a big-picture vision, and the drive
to make that vision a reality within the energy sector
●​ the ability to develop product requirements / roadmap based on input gathered from a variety
of sources, such as data analysis, feedback received from the user community, product
research, market research etc.


●​ the ability to define KPIs and develop key journeys depending on the KPIs and other
analysis.
